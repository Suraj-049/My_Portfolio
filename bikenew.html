Harley-Davidson, Inc. is an American motorcycle manufacturer founded in Milwaukee, Wisconsin in 1903.

It is one of the world's largest motorcycle manufacturers and has a rich history of producing iconic motorcycles known for their distinctive design and powerful performance.

Harley-Davidson motorcycles are often associated with a sense of freedom and rebellion, making them popular among motorcycle enthusiasts worldwide.
